# Autodoprava Kopeček — monetization

A lead-generation marketing site for a real transport/haulage business. The site
itself isn't monetized — it drives phone/contact leads that become jobs.

| Option | Likelihood of income | Possible earnings | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Site as a lead funnel → transport jobs** | High (indirect) | business revenue | The whole point; already built | Revenue is the business, not the site |
| **Google Business Profile visibility** | Medium (indirect) | more local leads | Free; strong local signal | Needs upkeep + reviews |
| **Local SEO / directory presence** | Low–Medium (indirect) | more leads | Compounding | Slow; ongoing effort |
| Ads on the site | n/a | — | — | Would cheapen a business site |
| Subscriptions | n/a | — | — | Not applicable |

**Recommendation:** optimise the site as a conversion-focused lead funnel and
keep the Google Business Profile current; no on-site ads or subscriptions.
